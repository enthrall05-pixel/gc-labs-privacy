Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: We don't see your conversations, we don't store your API keys, and your data goes directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider. Plictor is just the interface.

1. What Plictor is

Plictor is a browser-based interface that lets you interact with AI providers of your choice (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, and others) and connect to third-party services via MCP integrations. Plictor Work runs entirely in your browser. We do not proxy, store, or have access to your AI conversations.

2. Data we do not collect

  • Your AI conversations — these go directly from your browser to your chosen provider
  • Your API keys — stored in your browser's localStorage only, never transmitted to Plictor servers
  • Content from connected integrations (Gmail, Stripe, Notion, etc.)
  • Any files, documents, or data you work with through the agent

3. Data stored in your browser

Plictor Work stores the following locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Your selected AI provider name and model (not your API key value)
  • Which MCP integrations you have enabled
  • Your skills and preference settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Plictor Forge

Plictor Forge (the website builder at plictor.com/forge) uses Supabase for account management and stores your projects, templates, and usage data. Your account email and usage metrics are stored to support the product's credit system and billing.

5. Third-party providers

When you use Plictor Work with a cloud AI provider, your messages are sent directly to that provider under their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any provider you use:

  • Anthropic: anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: openai.com/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • xAI: x.ai/privacy

6. Analytics

We may collect basic, anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the product. No personally identifiable information is included.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Plictor after changes constitutes acceptance.
