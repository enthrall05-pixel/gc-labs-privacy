Simpterm

Last updated: April 2026

Privacy Policy

Simpterm stores SSH connection credentials locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

No data is sent to our servers. The only network connections made are the SSH connections you initiate to your own servers.

Data We Store Locally

  • SSH connection profiles (host, username, port)
  • SSH keys (stored in your device's secure storage)
  • App preferences

Data We DON'T Collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No usage data
  • No connection logs sent anywhere

SSH Connections

All SSH connections are made directly between your device and the servers you specify. We have no visibility into or control over these connections.

Permissions

  • Internet — Required for SSH connections
  • Storage — For SSH key files (if you choose to import them)

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at the email associated with your Google Play account.
