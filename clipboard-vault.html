Clipboard Vault

Last updated: April 2026

Privacy Policy

Clipboard Vault stores your clipboard data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data with external servers.

Data We Store

  • Clipboard history (text only) — stored locally using encrypted storage
  • App preferences (expiration settings, Pro status)

Data We DON'T Collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party analytics services
  • No personal information beyond your clipboard content

Biometric Authentication

If you enable biometric lock, authentication is handled entirely by your device's native biometric system. We never access or store your biometric data.

In-App Purchases

Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Permissions

  • Biometric — Optional, for securing your clipboard (Pro)
  • Internet — Required for in-app purchases only

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at the email associated with your Google Play account.
