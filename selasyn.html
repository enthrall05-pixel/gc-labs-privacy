Selasyn

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Selasyn does not store your conversations. Your messages are processed in memory and discarded. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

Who We Are

Selasyn is an AI-powered mental wellness companion available at selasyn.xyz and on Android via Google Play. It is operated by G/C Labs. Contact: hello@selasyn.xyz

What We Collect

We collect the minimum required to operate the service:

  • Device ID — a randomly generated identifier stored locally on your device. This ties your usage to your subscription tier (Free or Pro). It is not linked to your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Message content (in transit only) — your messages are sent to our server to generate AI responses. They are not logged or stored after processing.
  • Usage count — we track how many messages you've sent today to enforce the Free tier limit (20/day). This count resets daily.
  • Subscription status — if you upgrade to Pro, we store your device ID alongside your Stripe subscription status. No payment details are stored by us.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or account credentials
  • Your conversation history — messages are not stored on our servers
  • Location data
  • Device contacts, camera, microphone, or any device sensors
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any data shared with third parties for advertising

Local Storage (on your device)

The app stores the following locally on your device:

  • selasyn_device_id — your anonymous device identifier
  • selasyn_sessions — your chat history, stored locally on your device only, never uploaded

You can clear this at any time using the "Sign out" option in the app. Clearing this data removes your local session history and resets your device ID.

Payments (Pro Subscribers)

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. Stripe sends us a confirmation tied to your device ID to activate Pro features. For billing issues, contact hello@selasyn.xyz.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing. See stripe.com/privacy
  • AI model provider — message content is sent to our AI provider to generate responses. Messages are not used for model training without consent.

Data Retention

Conversation messages are not retained after generating a response. Usage counts reset daily. Device IDs are retained while a Pro subscription is active and removed on request.

Your Rights

  • Clear all local data using "Sign out" in the app
  • Request deletion of any server-side data tied to your device ID by emailing hello@selasyn.xyz
  • Contact us with any privacy questions at hello@selasyn.xyz

Children

Selasyn is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us at hello@selasyn.xyz and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The date at the top reflects the most recent update.
